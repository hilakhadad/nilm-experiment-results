■ Power Profile (20 pts) — Rewards clear low-power baseline (<100W). Penalizes 500-1000W stuck range. Score: 0-20.

■ Data Integrity (10 pts) — Penalties for NaN >1%, frequent gaps >5%, negative readings. Score: 0-10.
